WHO WE ARE

EDU7CAPITALS, powered by seven capitals, is a fintech platform pioneering towards making all the financial resources required to manage and run businesses and improve the quality of living of individuals with confidence.

Say goodbye to simple money saving. Hello, smart investing! In a world where technology is constantly evolving, lifestyles have progressed, and economies are developing at a fast pace, we have made it our top priority to assist you in adjusting to the future of seamless payments in the era of decentralized intermediaries and developing a distinctive investment mindset.

And with a distinctive cryptocurrency development program, we help companies with online investments, transactions, tokenization, and much more in the blockchain realm.

Through our advising program, we also assist small, medium, and big earners to maintain their income by recommending the most suited investment for them and enlightening them about their possibilities.

Likewise, we seek to eliminate financial illiteracy, which is the main contributor to bad financial decisions that have an impact on a variety of aspects of people's lives, including health, education, and retirement. Because it puts these people at a significantly higher risk of making poor financial decisions and, because those decisions have an impact on the economy as a whole, the lack of financial literacy especially among lower-income groups is a key issue that we wish to address and fix. To cater to these shortcomings, we’ve curated solutions for increasing one’s financial literacy including workshops and reading material on personal finance, and handheld sessions on how to invest in different money market instruments, among others.

MISSION
